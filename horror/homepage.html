Horror In The Woods

Wanting to escape the city you decided to rent a cabin out in the woods for a few nights. Nothing like seeing the stars and taking in the smog free air. You arrive at the cabin early in the morning and get settled. It’s not long until you’re off on a hike, breathing in nature and finally relaxing. Somewhere along your hike, the keys to the cabin dropped out of your pocket. You arrive back at the cabin as the sun is starting to set. There’s no cell service and the cabin owner wont be coming by until the morning. You’ve got to search for your keys…
